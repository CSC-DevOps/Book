DevOps: Concepts and Practice

By Dr. Christopher Parnin

Summary

In this book, you will gain practical exposure to the concepts, skills, tools, and knowledge needed in automating software engineering processes and infrastructure necessary for continuous deployment of software. You will understand how to configure computing environments, construct an automated deployment pipeline, operate and monitor infrastructure, and enable resilience of deployed software.
